Flexible and powerful Appointment Book
The highly configurable appointment book is the heart of PracSuite. It is packed full of features and powerful workflows that allow you to work efficiently while giving you a clear view of what is happening in your practice.

Automatic Appointment Reminders
Customise automatic SMS and Email reminders per business, practitioner, profession and appointment type.
Appointment Statuses
Appointments can automatically be marked as confirmed with an SMS reply from a patient. Statuses can manually be updated to mark patients as arrived, in session or did not attend.
Recurring appointments
Easily schedule a series of appointments.
Practitioner queue
Accessible from anywhere in PracSuite to view patients that are in the waiting room. Use the action menu to jump into patient tasks.
Drag and drop
To reschedule appointments and drag to adjust appointment durations.
Resources
Need to reserve equipment for an appointment? Allocate resources and receive alerts when the resource is unavailable at the time of booking or rescheduling an appointment.

Quickly view availability for coming weeks.

Rosters and availability
Manage week to week availability with ease, with options to override schedules with ad-hoc and recurring changes. Rosters also include tools to restrict certain types of appointments to certain times of the day.
Day View and Week view
Use day view to view availability across all business locations and profession. Week view displays availability for all practitioners in a single location for the whole week.
Multicolumn support
Need to fit multiple patients in the same time slot? Add multiple columns per practitioner at no additional cost.

Action menu
Managing patient appointments should be simple. The Action menu provides one click access to manage a patient appointment, whether it’s invoices, rescheduling, cancelling or managing a patient file.

Hand writing notes is also available on supported devices.

Detailed Billing
The advanced billing engine in PracSuite manages invoicing and payments for private patients, families, third-party payers, Medicare and DVA with ease. Manage real-world scenarios for patients with multiple accounts, referrers and treatment plans. Quickly issue invoices at the time of the consult or issue invoices and statements in bulk at the end of your billing cycle. PracSuite also integrates with third-party payment systems for seamless health fund and Medicare/DVA claiming and accepting credit card payments.
EXPLORE
Private Invoicing
Quickly and easily invoice and receive payment for patients whether they are paying out of pocket, via their health fund, Medicare or DVA.
Item Codes
Manage a list of billable item codes for both services and stock with support for setting fees per practitioner.
Third Party Payers
Bill to a third party and email invoice at the end of the week or month with support for multi-patient Invoicing.
Open Invoicing
Less clutter by combining multiple treatments over multiple days onto a single invoice.
Family Billing
Combine family members on a single invoice for quick and easy invoicing for an entire family.
Instant Emailing of Receipts and Invoices
One click to save and email invoices or receipts that sends in the background without delay.
Quantity Billing
Manage invoices billed on length of time more easily with quantity billing available up to two decimal places.
Bulk invoicing and statement generation
Print and email all unissued, overdue or outstanding invoices all at once based on individual client and third-party preferences from one easy to use report.
Fee Categories
Ensure your patients are charged the correct fee by defining fee structures for your business such as Private, Concession or Medicare.

Serious about Security
We are serious about security and data privacy. In addition to two-factor authentication, IP barring and access schedule restrictions, our highly configurable role-based permissions control what PracSuite users can do and what data they can access with all actions logged for auditing. For Australian subscribers, you will be happy to know that your data is stored in an enterprise-grade data centre within Australia. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and our systems undergo regular security scans to ensure a consitent and high level of security compliance.
